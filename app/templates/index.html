PoC of ENEA HeritageScience platform

This is the Proof of Concept of the ENEA HeritageScience platform, a microservices based tool to store, manage and visualize etherogeneus data from Cultural Heritage and heritage science activities and reasearchs.

This work is the result of the first ENEA Heritage Science 2021 Internship in collaboration with the Department of Computer, Control, and Management Engineering Antonio Ruberti (DIAG) of Sapienza University of Rome.

For more info about the software architecture, please refer to documentation in the software repository.

How to use it?

New Upload

This tool allow you to upload three kind of data:

  • Pubblications
  • Images
  • 3D Models
For each of these files, you have to provide some metadata compiling a simple form.

To upload files, go to New Upload section clicking on the navigation bar.

Search

Search the object you're looking for, querying the database.

Inventory

Get a complete list of all stored objects.

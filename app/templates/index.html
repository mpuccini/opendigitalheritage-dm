PoC of ENEA Open DigitalHeritage ecosystem

This is the Proof of Concept of the ENEA Open Digital Heritage ecosystem, a microservices based tool to store, manage and visualize etherogeneus data from Cultural Heritage and heritage science activities and reasearchs.

This work is the result of the first ENEA Heritage Science 2021 Internship in collaboration with the Department of Computer, Control, and Management Engineering Antonio Ruberti (DIAG) of Sapienza University of Rome.

For more info about the software architecture, please refer to documentation in the software repository.

How to use it?
  • inventoryInventory
    Get a complete list of all stored objects.
  • uploadNew Upload
    Upload your data. You can choose to upload pubblications, images and 3D models by filling in the provided forms for metadata.
  • searchSearch
    Make query on database with full text search support.
